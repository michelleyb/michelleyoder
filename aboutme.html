I'm a San Francisco based photographer that has loved being behind a camera for as long as I can remember. There is so much beauty in the world, I just want to capture it all. My favorite part of this job is showing people the beauty they may not have known they posess. In every smile and expression there exists a moment of perfect radiance. I always enjoy the happy responses I get when showing people the vision I captured.

I graduated from the Savannah College of Art and Design in 2006 with a bachelor's degree in fashion and photography and practically lived in the darkrooms throughout school. The smell of photo chemicals always brings me to my happy place. Although I now spend more time in front of a computer than surrounded by glowing red lights, I feel very lucky to have gotten into this art form when I did. Having this background really taught me how light and cameras work. I also love new technology and appreciate the ease of digital photography. I have become quite the Photoshop expert. I think this combination technical knowledge and digital mastery makes me really excel at what I do.

If you like what I do, feel free contact me. I would love to help make your projects come to life and capture the moments that you want to hold on to forever.
